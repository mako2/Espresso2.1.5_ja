Setting up Projects

To get the most out of Espresso, you can make a Project for your sites. This enables you to quickly find, edit and publish files. To set up a project:

  1. Choose Espresso > New Project…
  2. Either select "New Folder" for a brand new project, or "Existing Folder" to convert one of your sites to a project.
  3. When you're done creating the project, click Settings to configure your publishing servers.
